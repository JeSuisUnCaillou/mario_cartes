# v1.1.5 - Hit animation

## Hit animation

Currently, when a player is hit, he rotates in place. I want to add a cartoonish animation of a hit, with stars flying out. I added a hit_star asset for that.

Spawn 4 stars on hit, on the players position, rotated in 4 different angles for diversity. Tween them out rapidly while rotating them at the same time. They should travel roughly the same distance as the player's width, so they end up outside of his image.

Once their movement is over, they disapear.

This should happen for all hits, banana and shells.

Oh ! also the player's client should make his phone vibrate when he is hit.

## Dark mushroom animation

When a player is hit by a shell, we need to show that it generates a dark mushroom for the player, in the board. A dark mushroom should spawn on his position, and tween upwards (roughly the same distance as the player's width, so it end up outside of his image) while fading out. Then disapear.

## Objects destroy animation

When a shell hits a banana (or another shell) and they both get destroyed, instead of throwing them we are going to add a cartoon-ish animation of a dust cloud covering their disaperance.

The dust cloud should spawn on the position of the hit object, and grow from their size to double, while fading out.
