# v1.1.9 - Star

I want to add a new card : the star. I added the asset.

When a player plays a star, he becomes invincible until the start of his next turn. He can't be hit by any shells, can't be hit by bananas, nothing affects him.

The star is displayed on top of the player's avatar on the racetrack.

When the player enters a cell while star-invincible, he hits one of the items in the cell, in the same priority order than a shell : a player, or a banana, or a shell. It also hits like a shell : apply a slow counter on players, or destroy an item on the cell.
