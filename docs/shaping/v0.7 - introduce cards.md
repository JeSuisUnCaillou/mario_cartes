# v0.7 - Introduce cards

The game is played with a deck of cards.

On the player's screen (once he joined), we need to display three zones :

- Top zone : the editable name.
- middle zone : where you drage a card to play it
- bottom zone : where you have your cards

The bottom zone has 3 sub-zones :
- the small left zone : the draw pile (cards are face down).
- the large center zone : the current hand of cards, placed in a pretty fan. A hand is 5 cards max.
- the right zone : the discard pile (cards are face up).

The draw pile, current hand and discard pile must be persisted in the player's state in the back-end.

Each player starts with a deck of 8 cards. All the cards are the same : They say "Move forward 1 cell". The asset is in /assets/card - move forward.svg

All cards start face down in the draw pile. 
There is a "Draw" button on the draw pile when a player's hand is empty. When clicked, the player draws the top 5 cards of his draw pile. This happends server side, and the server sends to the client the ids of the specific cards he drew.

When he moves a card from his hand to the middle zone, it snaps here and stas for a second, to be played. It sends to the server a signal that this specific card has been played. The server resolves the action (move the player forward one cel) and moves the specific card from the hand to the discard pile.
