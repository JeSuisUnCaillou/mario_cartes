# v0.12 - Introducing modular cards

I want to rework the cards. Instead of static assets with banana, arrows and coins, I provided a blank card asset.

The server will build cards data with an amount of each following attributes :
- coin (gains a coin)
- banana (drops a banana)
- mushroom (moves forward) -> I made a new asset for the mushroom.

When the client is rendering a card, it builds it from the blank asset and adds the coin/banana/mushroom assets inside. A card can have up to 3 items in it.
- when there is one item in the card, it is placed in the middle of the card
- when there is two items in the card, they are placed top left and bottom right
- whene there is 3 items in the card, they are placed top left, middle irght, bottom left.

Space the items in the card evenly.

Every starting hand is :
- 3x a card with 1 coin
- 1x a card with 1 coin and one mushroom
- 1x a card with 1 banana, one coin and one mushroom
- 1x a card with 2 coins
- 1x a card with one banana
- 1x a card with one mushroom

(no more card with banana + move)
