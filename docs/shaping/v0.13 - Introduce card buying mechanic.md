# v0.13 - Introduce card buying mechanic

In the board UI, split the zone of the circtuit in two zones :
- on the left, the "river" zone
- on the right, the racetrack zone

The river zone is split into 3 zones on top of each other :
- the 1st river : cards cost 1 coin
- the second river : cards cost 3 coins
- the third river : cards cost 5 coins

Each river has a starting deck (like a player's draw pile), and 3 card slots.
On game start, each river "draws" 3 cards on its 3 slots. Each river's card has an id, (like the player's cards - they should if it's not the case yet).

On a player's turn, he can spend coins to buy cards from the rivers.

For that, we need a "Buy cards" button, which opens a modal with the 3 rivers and the player can choose a card to buy (if he has enough coins). Cards he can't buy (because not enough coins) are a bit transparent and grey.

When a card is bought, it is added to the player's discard pile with the usual discard animation (starting from the bought card position on the screen).

As usual, the server is the one responsible for the state of the game, and receives the event of "buy card" with the card's id and river id, and should check if the player is allowed to buy it. Then send the updated player state.
