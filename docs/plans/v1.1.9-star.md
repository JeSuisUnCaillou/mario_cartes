# v1.1.9 - Star

## Context

Adding the Star card to the game. The star item already exists in `assets/decks.yaml` (river_4: `2x star mushroom`) and the SVG asset exists at `assets/star.svg`. The server currently has no handler for the `star` item type, so playing a card containing `star` does nothing.

**Star behavior:** When played, the player becomes invincible until the start of their next turn. The invincibility persists through all other players' turns and is only cleared when the player's next turn begins (i.e. when they become the active player again). They can't be hit by any shells or bananas. When they enter a cell during movement, they hit one item on that cell using shell priority (player > banana > shell), applying a slow counter on players or destroying items.

**Design decisions:**
- Blue shell targeting a star-invincible player: no effect (animation plays, no discard)
- Star-invincible player hitting another player: slow counter only (like a shell hit)
