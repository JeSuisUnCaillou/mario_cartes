# v0.23 — Never skip a disconnected player's turn

## Problem

When a player disconnects during a game, their turn gets skipped in two ways:
1. `_advanceTurn()` skips over disconnected players in its loop condition
2. When the active player disconnects, a 10-minute timer auto-advances the turn

This means other players can play through while one player is temporarily disconnected, which is unfair.

## Solution

The game should **wait** on a disconnected player's turn until they reconnect. A disconnected player's turn is never skipped.

### Changes

1. **`_advanceTurn()`**: Remove `!player.connected` from the skip condition — only skip finished/ranked players
2. **`onLeave`**: Remove the 10-minute auto-advance timer entirely
3. **`onLeave` reconnection**: Remove timer cancellation code (no timer to cancel)

### Tests

New `disconnection.test.js` with:
- Turn lands on disconnected player and stays there (not skipped)
- Active player who disconnects keeps their turn (no auto-advance)
