Mario Cartes

A multiplayer racing card game. Mario Kart meets deckbuilders.

Or join an existing game by scanning its QR code on the big screen

How It Works

Put the board on a big screen

Open the game on a TV or laptop. It shows the track, player positions, and a QR code for everyone to join.

Players join on their phones

Scan the QR code to connect. Play cards, buy upgrades, throw shells — all from your phone.

1 Create the game on a big screen
2 Everyone scans the QR code
3 Race!
